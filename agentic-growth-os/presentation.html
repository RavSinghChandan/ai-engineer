🔍 What It Learns

It doesn't just run. It remembers — and decides.

🎯

Smarter Audience Targeting

If conversion rate is low, it automatically narrows the age range to the segment that actually converts — without you lifting a finger.

Example: 25–45 → refined to 28–40
✍️

Better Ad Copy Tone

Low ROI? Next run shifts the ad tone to create urgency. High CTR? It doubles down with testimonials and social proof.

Example: professional → urgent_cta
💰

Budget Goes Where It Converts

If Google Search outperforms Meta by 35%, budget automatically shifts in the next run. No manual dashboard diving.

Example: search budget 45% → 60%
📰

Headlines That Actually Hook

Low click rate? The system switches to question-hook headlines. Strong results? It uses the number-led approach that won last time.

Example: "Learn More" → "Tired of Paying Rent?"
